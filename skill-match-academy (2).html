Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you guarantee I will get accepted into a program?

A: No. We provide guidance and application support, but admissions decisions are made solely by the educational institutions.

Q: Can you guarantee I will get a job in Europe or Japan?

A: No. We provide information about pathways that may lead to international opportunities, but employment and visa outcomes depend on many factors beyond our control.

Q: What does the consultation include?

A: An assessment of your background and goals, an overview of relevant pathways, program recommendations, and a roadmap for next steps.

Q: Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

A: The first consultation is complimentary. Ongoing advisory services are subject to our professional fees, which will be discussed transparently.

A: We'll help you identify alternative options within Malaysia or discuss steps you can take to build eligibility over time.
